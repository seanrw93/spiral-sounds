Ordering

Browse the collection, add records to your cart, then proceed to checkout. We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. You'll need to create an account to complete a purchase — this lets you track your order history.

Shipping

Australian orders typically arrive within 3–7 business days. International orders take 10–21 business days depending on your country. We dispatch all orders within one business day of payment clearing.

Yes — free standard shipping on all Australian orders over $75. International orders are charged at cost based on weight and destination. You'll see the exact shipping fee at checkout before confirming your order.

Every record ships in a purpose-built cardboard record mailer with corner protectors and a stiffener board. We've shipped tens of thousands of records and take packaging seriously — we've never had a cracked sleeve complaint.

Record condition

We use the Goldmine grading standard: Mint (M), Near Mint (NM), Very Good Plus (VG+), Very Good (VG), and Good (G). Every record is graded by hand before listing. If there are any surface marks or sleeve wear worth knowing about, we note them in the listing description.

Both. We carry new sealed pressings, new open copies, and a curated selection of used records in excellent condition. The listing will always make clear whether a record is new or used, and the condition grade applies to both the vinyl and the sleeve.

Returns & problems

Email us within 7 days of delivery with a photo of the damage and your order number. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund — whichever you prefer. We cover return postage for damaged items.

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery on unopened records in original condition. Used records are sold as described — if you have a concern about a listing's accuracy, contact us and we'll sort it out. Return postage is the buyer's responsibility for change-of-mind returns.
