Our story

We believe every record tells a story.

Spiral Sounds is a Melbourne-based vinyl record store built for people who listen with intention. We source, curate, and ship records that are worth owning — not just collecting.

Where it started

It started in a spare bedroom with a turntable, a crate of second-hand jazz records, and a stubborn belief that digital streaming would never replace the ritual of putting on a record.

We opened our doors in 2019 with a handpicked catalogue of 200 records. Today we carry thousands — but we still approach every new addition the same way: would we want this in our own collection?

What we care about

Curation over quantity

We don't list everything we can find. We list what we'd genuinely recommend to a friend who trusts our taste.

Honest condition grading

We grade every record ourselves. If it's not near-mint we say so — and price it accordingly. No surprises.

Careful packaging

Every order is packed with record mailers and corner protectors. We've shipped tens of thousands of records without a single cracked sleeve complaint.

Built on recommendations

The majority of our new customers come from word of mouth. We'd rather earn your trust than advertise our way into it.

Browse the full collection or get in touch — we're always happy to help you track down something specific.
